About the Conference

The International Conference on Disruptive Technologies for Multi-Disciplinary Research & Applications aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of disruptive technologies and their applications across various disciplines.

This premier interdisciplinary platform provides opportunities to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields of Disruptive Technologies.
